◆ HOW IT WORKS

  1. 01 PREPROCESSING — Edge-based foreground detection isolates the utensil from the background. Image is cropped around the object's centre of mass.
  2. 02 FEATURE EXTRACTION — 6 custom geometric features are computed: elongation, roundness, width variation, top/bottom symmetry, and two template-matching scores.
  3. 03 CLASSIFICATION — A K-Nearest Neighbours classifier is trained and evaluated with 10-fold stratified cross-validation.
